About Me

Hello my name is Nnamdi Iroham. I emigrated from Nigeria at the age of 16 to Michigan. I was not too keen with winter, so after high school I decided to move to California for college. I have an 11 year old daughter Makaela and she is my entire universe. My passion include poetry, writing short stories and with that I was able to publish a book titled Balance Keys: 7 insightful impacts on Amazon Kindle. I love the guitar from my love for djaying, mainly scratching records (elements of hip hop). Coding is something I have totally embraced and love as we continue to navigate to become great coders and programmers.
